Brief Profile of the Directors

                                                                
                                                      Mr. Md. Iftikhar-uz-zaman, Chairman   
  Mr. Md. Iftikhar-uz-zaman, Deputy Managing Director of Investment Corporation of  Bangladesh (ICB), has joined ICB Asset Management Company Ltd as the Chairman  of the Board of Directors on January 19, 2015. He joined as Senior Officer of  Investment Corporation of Bangladesh in 1983 and held important positions in  Economic and Business Research, Transactions, Public Issue, Project Loan, Project  Implementation, Personnel, Loan Appraisal, Administration and Legal Affairs  Divisions  of ICB in different capacities. He was CEO of ICB Capital Management  Limited (ICML) from March 08, 2004 to November 26, 2008 until he was promoted to  General Manager in the year 2008 and joined as Director of ICML on 14 February,  2010. Before joining as the Deputy Managing Director of ICB he also served as the  Deputy Managing Director of Janata Bank Ltd. He attended many workshops in home  & abroad during his career.

Dr. Mijanur Rahman, Director
  Dr. Mijanur Rahman, one of the directors of the Company, is the  present Vice Chancellor (VC) of the Jagannath University. Before  joining as the VC, he served as the Professor of Marketing and the  Treasurer of the University of Dhaka. He did his B.Com (Hons) and  M.Com degree in Marketing from the University of Dhaka and  obtained his Ph.D. degree in Business Administration from Aligarh  Muslim University, India. He has completed different training in home  and abroad. He has various publications and attended national and  international workshop/seminar.

Mr. Md. Jahangir Miah, Director
  Mr. Md. Jahangir Miah is an MA from the University of Exeter,  England. Earlier he completed his Masters in Management from the  University of Chittagong. He is an experienced investment banker, former Deputy Managing Director of Janata Bank Ltd and the Chief  Executive Officer of Janata Capital and Investment Limited. He  served as General Manager of Investment Corporation of  Bangladesh  (ICB). He also served as Chief Executive Officer of ICB  Asset   Management Company Limited and ICB Capital Management  Company Ltd, and Chairman of ICB Securities Trading Company  Limited. Besides he worked as nominated director in various listed  and non¬listed companies including Islami Bank Bangladesh Limited  and Unique Hotels and Resorts Limited, The Westin, Dhaka.

Mr. MD. Alauddin Khan, Director
  Mr. Md. Alauddin Khan, B.Com (Hons) and M.Com in Finance from  the University of Dhaka. He is Deputy General Manager of ICB and  has attended several training programs in home and abroad. He has  completed 31 years of service in the field of investment banking. He  is  the former Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of ICB Asset Management  Company Limited. ICB has appointed him as the Director of ICB  AMCL on April 06, 2015 in place of Mr. Mahmud Mizanur Rahman  who  has been appointed as the CEO of ICB Capital Management Ltd.

Mr. Md. Nazrul Islam Khan, Director & CEO
  Mr. Md. Nazrul Islam Khan, B.Com (Hons) and M.Com in Finance  from the University of Dhaka. He is Deputy General Manager of ICB  and has attended several training programs in home and abroad. He  has completed 30 years of service in the field of investment banking.  Presently he has been appointed as the Chief Executive Officer (CEO)  of ICB Asset Management Company Limited on April 05, 2015.
